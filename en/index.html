Our Solutions

G-SMART – Smart Drone Control Kit

Use Cases

One System. Endless Applications.

DRONER operates in all weather and every scenario – from emergency response to routine maintenance. The platform is flexible, customizable, and based on G-DOCK, G-SMART, and G-CONTROL systems.

Smart Security

Autonomous patrols, event response, suspicious movement detection, and full documentation – 24/7, even without on-site personnel.

Fire Safety & Early Detection

Planned patrols for smoke detection, abnormal heat measurement, early warning, and real-time disaster prevention.

Public Order & Traffic Monitoring

Aerial observation at events, crowd and vehicle tracking, recording for documentation, and field force guidance.

Operational Supervision & Control

Monitoring construction sites, infrastructure, work areas, and warehouses – improving processes and preventing faults.

Aerial Deliveries

Delivery between secure sites (such as medical or tech equipment), in accordance with BVLOS requirements and proper licensing.

High-Rise Cleaning

Integrated drone for cleaning building facades from the roof – no cranes, rappelling, or height-dependent personnel.

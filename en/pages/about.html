G-SYSTEM – The Company Behind DRONER

Meet the structure, experience, and people that drive our operational excellence at the forefront of drone technology.

Who We Are

G-SYSTEM is the company behind the flagship product, DRONER. Founded in 2019, we specialize in the development, deployment, and operation of autonomous drone systems for aerial security, remote monitoring, and control.

With years of experience and technological innovation, G-SYSTEM delivers advanced solutions that combine the DRONER system, smart docking stations, and professional operational services. We are committed to excellence, reliability, and innovation, leading the autonomous drone sector in Israel.

What We Do

G-SYSTEM designs, installs, and operates autonomous aerial security solutions, including advanced drones, smart docking stations, remote management systems, and a 24/7 manned control center. We collaborate with leading international technology partners, including:

  • HEISHA – A world leader in autonomous drone docking stations, providing full autonomy for drones, enabling advanced mission deployment over time, and tailored for complex operational environments.
  • FlightOps – An advanced technology company offering a unique platform for remote management and control of large, autonomous drone fleets. FlightOps technology enables remote operation without range limitations, fully integrated with DRONER’s operational and control systems.

These partnerships provide DRONER clients with integrated, high-quality solutions emphasizing innovation, reliability, and operational precision.

Why DRONER?

  • Cutting-edge Technology – Integrating advanced systems from HEISHA and FlightOps for autonomous security and monitoring solutions.
  • Proven Operational Reliability – Stable, robust, and highly available systems for all weather and field conditions.
  • Operational Efficiency & Savings – Reduced manpower, insurance, and operational risk costs.
  • Deep Experience & Expertise – A professional, operational, and engineering team with extensive background in drone and advanced control systems.

Our Team

Our professional team consists of experts in diverse fields – from drone piloting, through autonomous system design and integration, to technological and operational management. We work to the highest standards of quality, safety, and reliability to deliver perfect, tailored solutions for our clients.

Our Vision

Our vision at G-SYSTEM is to lead the autonomous drone sector in Israel, providing our clients with a significant technological advantage, full field control, and the ability to respond quickly and efficiently to any security, operational, or business challenge.

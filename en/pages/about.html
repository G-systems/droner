About

Advanced Autonomous Drone Solutions for Security and Monitoring

Who We Are

DRONER was founded from the merger of two leading companies:

G-System
Experts in aerial security and drones since 2019
TCM Technologies
Specializing in operational deployment and integrative engineering of drone systems

The merger created an advanced professional array specializing in the deployment of autonomous drone systems and remote control systems at client sites, with an emphasis on security, monitoring, and advanced control.

What We Do

DRONER designs, installs, and operates autonomous aerial security solutions including advanced drones, smart docking stations, remote management and control systems, and a 24/7 manned control center. The company works in strategic technological collaboration with leading international companies, including:

  • HEISHA – A leading manufacturer of autonomous docking stations, considered one of the world's top providers of automated drone charging and storage solutions. HEISHA's docking stations provide full autonomy for drones, enabling advanced mission deployment over time, and are especially suited for complex operational environments.
  • FlightOps – An advanced technology company providing a unique platform for remote management and control of large, autonomous drone fleets. FlightOps' technology enables remote operation and control without range limitations, fully integrating with DRONER's operational and control systems.

These collaborations provide DRONER's clients with integrated, high-quality solutions that emphasize innovation, reliability, and operational precision.

Why DRONER?

  • Cutting-edge Technology – Integrating advanced systems from HEISHA and FlightOps for autonomous security and monitoring solutions.
  • Proven Operational Reliability – Stable, durable, and highly available systems for all weather and terrain conditions.
  • Cost Savings & Operational Efficiency – Reducing manpower, insurance costs, and operational risk.
  • Experience & Deep Expertise – A professional, operational, and engineering team with extensive background and experience in drone and advanced control systems.

Our Team

Our professional team consists of experts in diverse fields – from drone piloting, through planning and integration of autonomous systems, to technological and operational management. The team works to the highest standards of quality, safety, and reliability to provide our clients with perfect, tailor-made solutions.

Our Vision

DRONER's vision is to lead the field of autonomous drones in Israel and provide our clients with a significant technological advantage, full field control, and the ability to respond quickly and efficiently to any challenge – security, operational, or mission-related.

Our Clients

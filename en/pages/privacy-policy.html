Privacy Policy

Here you will find all information about privacy and data protection on the DRONER website

What information do we collect?

  • Information you provide directly (e.g., when contacting us through the website or registering for services).

  • Information collected via cookies and analytics tools.

How do we use the information?

  • To improve the user experience on the site.
  • To provide services, respond to inquiries, and offer technical support.

Data Retention and Security

We take reasonable and accepted security measures to protect your personal information. However, we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

Sharing Information with Third Parties

We do not share personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • To comply with legal requirements or a court order.

  • To service providers who assist us in operating the website (subject to confidentiality obligations).

Cookies and Third-Party Tools

The site uses cookies and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) for operation, security, and service improvement. You can change your browser settings to block cookies, but some functions may not work properly.

Links to External Sites

The site may contain links to external websites. We have no control over the privacy policies of these sites, and we recommend reviewing their policies separately.

DJI Dock 2 & DJI Dock 3

Autonomous docking stations by DJI for DRONER – full integration, high reliability, easy control

DJI's Official Solution for Autonomous Operation

The DJI Dock 2 and DJI Dock 3 are advanced autonomous docking stations designed for organizations that require reliability, full integration with DJI drones, and simple operation. The stations enable remote control, automatic charging, scheduled operation, and a user-friendly management interface – all under the warranty and service of an official importer.

  • Full compatibility with DJI Matrice 3D/3TD and Matrice 4D Series drones
  • Simple operation, quick installation, and user-friendly interface
  • Service and support from an official importer in Israel
  • High reliability, minimal maintenance
